About Talakbay

Talakbay is a thesis project developed by Gotcha! Productions. It combines a Cinematic Instructional Video and a Structured Photowalk Program designed to help Grade 12 Arts and Design Track students improve their photography skills through guided experiential learning.

🎬 Cinematic Instructional Video

A visual learning material that presents photography concepts and techniques through cinematic storytelling.

📸 Structured Photowalk Program

A guided photography activity where participants apply concepts through hands-on practice, reflection, and feedback.

🌐 Supplementary Website

An online resource platform that provides additional photography materials and references.
