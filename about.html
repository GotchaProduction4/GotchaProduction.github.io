About Lakbay Pa-Tala

Lakbay Pa-Tala is a thesis project developed by Gotcha! Productions. It explores photography learning through a Cinematic Instructional Video and a Structured Photowalk Program designed for Grade 12 Arts and Design Track students.


📸 Cinematic Instructional Video

A visual learning material that introduces photography concepts, techniques, and creative approaches through cinematic storytelling.

🚶 Structured Photowalk Program

A guided experiential photography activity where students apply concepts through hands-on practice, reflection, and feedback.
