📸 Photowalk Basics

Learn how to conduct a proper photowalk

📍 What is a Photowalk?

A photowalk is when photographers walk around a location and take photos while learning composition, lighting, and storytelling.

🎯 How to Conduct a Photowalk

  • Choose a location (parks, streets, city areas)
  • Bring your camera or phone
  • Focus on lighting (morning or sunset is best)
  • Try different angles
  • Take group or solo shots
